Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement is the focus of content within a webpage, and the enchancment of the website using the basics (HTML, CSS, and Java). Another way to word progressive enchancement is the seperation of HTML, CSS, and Java. If the first basic step, markup, it not set up right, then the rest of the steps would not create a functioning website. The importantance of progressive enhancement is to create functioning websites without having to constantly maintain it, and to also check your work before continuing to the next step to making a website.
